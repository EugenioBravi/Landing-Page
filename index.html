Places to visit in Germany

Germany boasts some of the most impressive nature destinations in Europe, most of which are accessed by hiking through the countryside or crossing alpine lakes by boat.

The best places in Germany

Basteibrücke, or Bastion Bridge, is a medieval-era construction located in the Saxon Switzerland National Park.

Externsteine is a series of sandstone columns jutting out of Wiembecke, a natural lagoon in the Teutoburg Forest.

Obersee is a crystal-clear fjord located in the Berchtesgaden National Park, about 140 kilometers southeast of Munich.

Rakotzbrücke is a masonry bridge located in the azalea and rhododendron park.
